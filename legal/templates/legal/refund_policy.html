Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2025
Effective Date: June 26, 2025

1. Introduction

LS SPECIAL PROJECTS PTY LTD trading as ESJ Sport Solutions operates the SAFA Global Management System. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on purchases made through our platform.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 SAFA Merchandise

✅ Eligible for Refund
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Incorrect items shipped
  • Items not as described
  • Unused items within 30 days
  • Items with manufacturing defects
❌ Not Eligible for Refund
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Items worn or used
  • Items without original packaging
  • Digital products (after download)
  • Items purchased on sale (final sale)

2.2 Registration and Service Fees

⚠️ Important Notice

Registration fees, membership fees, and service fees are generally non-refundable except in the following circumstances:

  • System error resulted in duplicate payments
  • Service was not provided due to technical issues
  • SAFA policy changes affect membership validity
  • Duplicate registrations within 24 hours

2.3 Event Tickets

  • Full Refund: Event cancellation by SAFA
  • Partial Refund: Event postponement (processing fees may apply)
  • No Refund: Customer cancellation (subject to terms)
  • Transfer: Tickets may be transferred to future events

3. Refund Timeframes

Product/Service Type Refund Period Conditions
Physical Merchandise 30 days from delivery Unused, original packaging
Digital Products 7 days from purchase Not downloaded/accessed
Registration Fees 48 hours from payment Before processing begins
Event Tickets As per event terms Subject to event policy
Membership Fees 14 days from payment Services not yet utilized

4. How to Request a Refund

4.1 Step-by-Step Process

  1. Contact Support: Email refunds@esjsport.com or call +27 (0) 11 XXX XXXX
  2. Provide Details: Order number, reason for refund, supporting documentation
  3. Await Review: We will review your request within 2-3 business days
  4. Return Items: If applicable, return items using provided instructions
  5. Receive Refund: Refund will be processed once approved

4.2 Required Information

When requesting a refund, please provide:

  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Photos (if item is damaged or defective)
  • Proof of purchase
  • Contact information

5. Refund Processing

5.1 Processing Times

  • Review: 2-3 business days
  • Approval: 1-2 business days
  • Refund to Original Payment Method: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Cash Refund: Available at office locations

5.2 Refund Methods

Original Payment Method

Refunded to the original card or account used for payment

Bank Transfer

Direct transfer to your nominated bank account

Store Credit

Credit towards future purchases (when applicable)

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following situations:

  • Items returned without original packaging
  • Items showing signs of use
  • Items returned after the refund period
  • Processing fees for certain transactions
  • Restocking fees for special orders

7. Non-Refundable Items and Services

❌ The following are generally non-refundable:
  • Personalized jerseys and equipment
  • Digital membership cards (once issued)
  • Completed registration processing
  • Certification fees (once certificates are issued)
  • Training course fees (once commenced)
  • Administrative and processing fees
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

8. Return Shipping

  • Defective Items: We cover return shipping costs
  • Customer Returns: Customer pays return shipping
  • Wrong Item Sent: We cover all shipping costs
  • Change of Mind: Customer pays return shipping

9. Disputes and Escalation

If you are not satisfied with our refund decision:

  1. Request a review from our Customer Service Manager
  2. Escalate to our Director of Operations
  3. Contact the Consumer Protection Commission if necessary
  4. Seek independent mediation if required
