Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2025

1. Introduction

LS SPECIAL PROJECTS PTY LTD trading as ESJ Sport Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SAFA Connect Membership System. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, ID number
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, biographical information
  • Membership Data: SAFA registration details, certification status
  • Transaction Information: Purchase history, payment details (encrypted)

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information for service improvement
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues
  • To process payments and prevent fraud
  • To generate digital membership cards and QR codes

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 SAFA Integration

As this is the official SAFA registration system, certain information is shared with the South African Football Association for:

  • Player and official registration
  • Membership verification
  • Competition eligibility
  • Statistical reporting

4.2 Service Providers

We may employ third party companies and individuals to facilitate our service. These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Document watermarking for protection

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing in certain circumstances

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce legal agreements
  • Maintain SAFA registration records

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your country where data protection laws may differ. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. For users under 18, parental consent is required for registration.

10. PWA and Offline Data

Our Progressive Web App may store data locally on your device for offline functionality. This data is encrypted and synchronized when you reconnect to the internet.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
