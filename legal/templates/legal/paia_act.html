5. How to Request Information

5.1 Request Process

  1. Complete Form: Use the official PAIA request form (Form C)
  2. Submit Request: Send to our Information Officer
  3. Pay Fees: Pay applicable fees (if any)
  4. Wait for Response: We will respond within 30 days

5.2 Request Requirements

Your request must include:

  • Sufficient details to identify the record(s)
  • Proof of identity
  • Reason for the request (if personal information of another person)
  • Preferred form of access
  • Contact details

7. Grounds for Refusal

We may refuse access to information if:

  • It would violate another person's privacy
  • It contains trade secrets or commercial information
  • It could harm the commercial interests of the organization
  • It is protected by legal professional privilege
  • It could endanger someone's safety
  • It would violate confidentiality agreements

8. Appeal Process

If your request is refused, you may:

  1. Lodge an internal appeal with our Information Officer
  2. Apply to the Information Regulator
  3. Approach the High Court

9. Protection of Personal Information (POPIA)

PAIA must be read together with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We are committed to protecting personal information in accordance with both Acts.
